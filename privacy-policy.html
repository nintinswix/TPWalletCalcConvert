Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy — no data collection

Summary

TpŵalłetCalcConvert is a browser-based productivity extension that helps users generate sample text, count words/characters/sentences, and convert text cases (uppercase, lowercase, title case, and sentence case). We value user privacy and transparency. This policy explains exactly how your data is handled, why permissions are required, and how the extension operates.

Data Collection

We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information. This includes:

  • Names, email addresses, or any other identifying information
  • Browsing history or visited URLs
  • Keystrokes or typed content (unless copied intentionally by the user)
  • Analytics, usage tracking, or crash reports

All processing of text occurs locally in your browser. The extension never sends your text to any server unless you explicitly choose to copy and paste it somewhere.

How the Extension Works

All features run entirely on your device:

  • Text Generation: Lorem ipsum paragraphs are generated locally.
  • Word Counter: Word, character, and sentence counts are calculated in real-time on your device.
  • Case Converter: Text transformations happen immediately within the browser, without external calls.

This ensures that no external servers are involved and your data stays private.
